What is an SSL Certificate?

An SSL (Secure Socket Layer) certificate is a digital certificate that establishes the online identity and security of a website. An SSL certificate puts the “S” in https, making your site secure and trusted by customers.

Benefits of SSL

  • Web security best practices

  • Protects confidential data

  • Improved customer trust

  • Boosted Google ranking
